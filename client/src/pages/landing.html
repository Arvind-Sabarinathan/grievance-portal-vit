About the Portal

The VIT Grievance Redressal Portal is dedicated to providing a transparent, fair, and efficient system for addressing student and faculty concerns. Our goal is to foster an environment where issues are heard, assessed, and resolved promptly.

How It Works

  1. Submit: Raise your grievance through our simple online form.
  2. Review: Your concern is reviewed by the relevant authority.
  3. Resolve: Get notified once the grievance is resolved.
